About Us

Our Story

F&T Construction & Supply is a licensed contractor accredited by the Philippine Contractors Accreditation Board (PCAB), Republic of the Philippines (License No. 21943).

Established in 1996 by a consortium of highly skilled technical professionals with extensive local and international experience in construction and project management, the company has built a reputation for reliability, integrity, and quality workmanship.

The combined talents, expertise, and dedication of our management and technical teams form a strong, synergized organization committed to delivering world-class construction solutions and consistently exceeding client expectations.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction services that exceed client expectations through innovative solutions, quality materials, and skilled workmanship.

Our Vision

To be the leading construction company recognized for reliability, integrity, and excellence in every project we undertake.
